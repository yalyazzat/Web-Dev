Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1 pound
Parmesan 1 cup

Step 1: Heat the pasta water. Put a large pot of salted water on to boil (1 tablespoon salt for every 2 quarts of water.)

Step 2: Cook the pasta. Once the water has reached a rolling boil, add the dry pasta, and cook, uncovered, at a rolling boil.

Step 3: Add parmesan to pasta.

Pizza Margarita

  • Time: 25
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Bread flour 300g
Ball mozzarella 125g

Step 1: Make the base: Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt. Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough.

Step 2: Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.

Latter

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 30g
Coffee 20g

Step 1: Brew an espresso to taste.

Step 2: While the coffee is brewing, heat up some milk in the microwave or in a non-stick pan on the hob (use 3 times the volume of your espresso).

Step 3: Whisk the milk as fast as you can, using a handheld whisk (a fork will do). If possible, use a small battery-operated whisk for extra froth.
